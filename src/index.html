Quick start guide

Welcome to LiveU, an easy to use no sign up required audio, video and text chat with built in end to end privacy over a peer to peer (no middle man) network

To start your own room:

Step 1: Please click "allow" for both camera and microphone when the prompt is shown.
Step 2: Either click the Dice button for a random room name or input your own room name, this must be atleast 6 characters

Please Note: anyone with your room name can enter your room, if you would like to ensure your privacy, please use the dice button or input a unique name and communicate it only to your partners
Step 3: Click Start
Step 4: Click copy link and share it with who you would like to invite to your room

Once you are in the chat room the following commands can be found at the bottom menu, their functions are as follows:

Settings
Enable/Disable Video Streaming
Enable/Disable Audio Streaming
Record/Stop Recording
Share your desktop
Share a file
Show text chat window
Leave the call
